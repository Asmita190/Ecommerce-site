Research on how the website is influenced
1.Image Combination
I liked how ebay.com placed their image on the center, including texts on it and some buttons too with some background images, gradient colors. Hence, I did the same to my website put on some background images with products on it. Also used the texting style and button style with hover effect.[Click the Picture to go directly into the website]
2.Color Combination
The color combination of marie forleo.com was so eye-catching. and the logo in the bars along with page names were so beautifully created. I too wanted to do the same and matched some pastel colors and on the bars kept a cart button on the right side .[Click the Picture to go directly into the website]
