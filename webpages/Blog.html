Impacts of technology in our daily life

          Technology is changing the way people communicate, learn and think. It helps society and determines how people interact with each other on a daily basis. Technology plays an important role in modern society. It has a positive and negative impact on the world and affects everyday life. We live in an age where technological progress is commonplace. The Internet and cell phones are some examples. However, with the development of technology, all this has a downside.

One aspect of technology that has had a tremendous impact on society is how it affects learning. It has made learning more interactive and collaborative, which helps people better engage with the material they are learning and struggling with. to resources. With the creation of the Internet, it allows us to access information at a speed of 24 hours, and you have access to almost everything online. In addition, it makes the students' work easier.Students can take quizzes and tests more easily, and the ability for teachers to teach classes online can be very effective. It also pushes the boundaries of the classroom and encourages self-paced learning. People can access the learning through YouTube and social networks. This helps students learn better. they sit for lectures and read textbooks. These technological advances made learning more fun and convenient.

Another possibility in which technology is affected by the company is the communication as we speak with the other world and communicate.There is better access to treatment, there is a change in medical care that benefits the elderly, and hospitals are using advanced technology in their operating rooms.

However, studies show that mobile communication negatively affects people's ability to be social and connect personally. Mobile technology can affect communication and relationships between people. There is less personal time where you find that you don't have enough time for yourself because you are always in touch with someone. Also, it can distract you from your school work. Also, privacy is lost because anyone can find you anywhere and at any time of the day.In summary, all of these things affect the way people act today. Without technological advances, our way of life would not be so complex. Technological influences characterize the actions of people today.
